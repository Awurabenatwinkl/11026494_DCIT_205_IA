Hello Elite.

Welcome to the Computer Science Department, where innovation meets education. Our dedicated faculty and cutting-edge curriculum are designed to empower students like you with the knowledge and skills needed to thrive in the ever-evolving world of technology. Explore the exciting opportunities and challenges that await you on your journey through the diverse and dynamic field of computer science.

Some of the courses offered

Mobile Application development

We provide world class technologies for Mobile Application Development using the mobile-first worklight by IBM.

Introduction To Web Programming

Understand the fundamentals of web technologies and create web-based applications with latest technologies.

Computer networks

We provide quality training for students to become a professional network analyst.

Databases

Master the art of database management in our computer science course, gaining expertise in organizing and optimizing data for seamless information retrieval.

Atificial Intelligence

Embark on an intellectual journey into artificial intelligence, unlocking the potential of machines to learn, adapt, and solve complex problems in our computer science course.

Internet of Things

Explore the interconnected world of the Internet of Things (IoT) in our computer science course, where you'll discover how devices communicate and collaborate to enhance efficiency and connectivity.

Digital and Electronic circuit

Delve into the realm of digital and electronic circuits in our computer science course, mastering the foundations that drive modern computing and electronic systems.

3D Game development

Immerse yourself in the world of 3D game development, unlocking creative possibilities

Computer organization and architecture
